# **App Name**: Fjármál Land

## Core Features:

- Entry Registration: Record sales and expenses with date, category, and amount.
- Invoice Generation: Create invoices compliant with Icelandic law, incorporating company details (name, logo URL, kenitala) from the settings.
- Multilingual Support: Switch seamlessly between Icelandic, English, Spanish, and Polish.
- Profit & Loss Calculation: Automatically compute profit and loss statements based on registered entries.
- Settings Configuration: Customize the app with company name, logo URL, and kenitala (Icelandic identification number).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, professional blue (#336699), reminiscent of Icelandic landscapes.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue-gray (#E8F0FE), providing a clean and professional feel.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#5F9EA0), complementing the primary blue while adding a touch of vibrancy.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font providing a clean and neutral reading experience for all text in the application.
- Use clear and professional icons for navigation and entry categories.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout for ease of use.
- Subtle transitions when switching between languages or displaying reports.
